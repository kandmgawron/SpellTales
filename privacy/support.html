Frequently Asked Questions

Does SpellTales collect personal data?

No. SpellTales is designed for children and does not collect any personal information. We follow strict COPPA and GDPR-Kids standards.

Why do I see ads?

We show only non-personalised, child-safe ads through Google AdMob. Ads help keep the app free for families.

How do I report a story issue?

If a story appears incomplete or unusual, please email us with your device type and the details so we can improve the app.

Can I request new features?

Absolutely! We welcome suggestions from parents and educators. Send your ideas to us any time.

About SpellTales

SpellTales is a safe, educational storytelling app designed to help children learn new words and spark their imagination through personalised stories.
