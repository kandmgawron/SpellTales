SpellTales – Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 18, 2025

SpellTales (“the App”, “we”, “our”, “us”) is a children’s story-creation application designed for safe use by children under the age of 13. We take privacy extremely seriously and are committed to protecting the personal information of every child and parent who uses the App.

1. Information We Collect

SpellTales does not require any personal information from users. Children can use the App without providing names, emails, or any identifying details.

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

The App uses Google AdMob to display non-personalised, child‑safe ads. AdMob may collect limited non‑identifying information such as:

  • Product interaction data
  • Advertising data (non‑personalised)
  • Crash data
  • Performance data

No identifiers (IDFA), tracking data, precise location, analytics, or personalised advertising data are collected.

2. How We Use Information

We use non‑personal data only to ensure ads function correctly, diagnose technical issues, and improve performance.

3. Third‑Party Services

We use Google AdMob in COPPA‑compliant, non‑personalised mode. AdMob’s privacy policy is available at: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

4. Children’s Privacy

SpellTales is designed for children under 13 and fully complies with COPPA and GDPR‑Kids requirements. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we may have collected such information, contact us and we will remove it immediately.

5. Data Retention

We do not store any personally identifying information. Non‑personal technical data is retained only by AdMob according to their own policies.

6. Data Security

We use secure infrastructure and industry‑standard protections provided by Apple, Google, and AWS. No personal data is stored on our servers.

7. Your Rights

Because we do not collect personal data, most data subject rights (access, correction, deletion) do not apply. Contact us with questions.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the App or regulations.
