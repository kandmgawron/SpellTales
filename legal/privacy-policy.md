# Privacy Policy for SpellTales

**Last Updated: January 14, 2025**

## Introduction

SpellTales ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (for account creation and authentication)
- Password (encrypted and stored securely via AWS Cognito)
- Child profile names and ages (stored locally and in our secure database)

### Story Data
- Characters, keywords, and story preferences you select
- AI-generated stories (saved to your account)
- Learning words you add for educational purposes

### Usage Information
- App usage patterns and feature interactions
- Error logs and crash reports (for app improvement)
- Device type and operating system version

### Biometric Data
- Face ID/Touch ID authentication data (stored locally on your device only, never transmitted)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Create and manage your account
- Generate personalized AI stories for your children
- Save and retrieve your story history
- Provide age-appropriate content filtering
- Process subscription payments
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Send important service updates

## Data Storage and Security

- **Authentication**: Managed by AWS Cognito with industry-standard encryption
- **Story Storage**: Stored in AWS DynamoDB with encryption at rest
- **Local Storage**: Profile data and preferences stored securely on your device
- **Biometric Data**: Never leaves your device; handled by iOS secure enclave

## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

SpellTales is designed for children aged 2-17 under parental supervision. We:
- Do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children
- Require parental account creation and management
- Do not share children's data with third parties
- Allow parents to review and delete all child-related data
- Use AI content filters to ensure age-appropriate stories

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your stories for AI training
- Share data with third parties except as required by law

We DO share data with:
- AWS (our cloud infrastructure provider) - for app functionality
- Apple (for in-app purchases and subscriptions)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Export your story data
- Opt out of non-essential communications

## Data Retention

- Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
- Deleted accounts: All data permanently deleted within 30 days
- Stories: Retained until you delete them or close your account

## Advertising

- Free users see advertisements served by Google AdMob
- Premium subscribers have an ad-free experience
- We do not use your personal data for targeted advertising

## Data Location

Your data is stored in the United Kingdom (AWS EU-West-2 London region). We use AWS infrastructure located in the UK to ensure data sovereignty and compliance with UK and EU data protection regulations.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.
