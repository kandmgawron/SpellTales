# Privacy Policy for Bedtime Stories App

**Last updated: November 11, 2025**

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (for account creation and authentication)
- Password (encrypted and stored securely)

### Content Data
- Custom learning words you add
- Story generation preferences (characters, genres, keywords)
- Generated stories (stored locally on your device)

### Usage Data
- App usage patterns for improving functionality
- Error logs for debugging purposes

## How We Use Your Information

- **Story Generation**: To create personalized bedtime stories using AI
- **Learning Features**: To incorporate your custom learning words into stories
- **Account Management**: To authenticate and manage your account
- **Service Improvement**: To enhance app functionality and user experience

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- Account data stored securely in AWS Cognito
- Learning words stored in AWS DynamoDB with encryption
- Stories generated on-demand and can be stored locally on your device

## Third-Party Services

- **AWS Services**: For secure data storage and AI story generation
- **Advertising**: Free users may see ads from third-party networks
- **Analytics**: Anonymous usage data to improve the app

## Children's Privacy

This app is designed for children's content but requires adult supervision for account setup and management. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

## Your Rights

- Access your stored data
- Delete your account and associated data
- Opt out of non-essential data collection
